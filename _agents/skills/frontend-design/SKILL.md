This skill guides the creation of minimalist, high-end interfaces inspired by Apple's industrial design language. It prioritizes clarity, generous negative space, and refined sensory details.

## Design Thinking: Apple-Inspiration
- **Simplicity as Sophistication:** Remove every unnecessary line, border, and color.
- **Negative Space:** Use vast, deliberate whitespace to create focus.
- **Refined Typography:** Use clean sans-serifs (like SF Pro, Inter, or Outfit) with meticulous kerning and line heights.
- **Soft Glassmorphism:** Implement frosted glass effects (`backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`) that feel expensive and subtle.
- **Squircular Geometry:** Use smooth, varied border radii (24px to 40px) that mimic industrial hardware.

## Execution
- **Color Palette:** Pure whites, deep blacks (#000000), and soft grays (#f5f5f7 / #1d1d1f). One single, vibrant accent color (e.g., Apple Blue #007aff).
- **Motion:** Fluid, ease-out transitions. Scroll-triggered fades and elegant staggered reveals. No jitters.
- **Imagery:** High-contrast, studio-quality product visuals or minimalist geometric metaphors.

CRITICAL: Avoid clutter. Elegance comes from what you LEAVE OUT. Maintain a distinctive character through meticulous attention to detail and unexpected subtle refinements.
